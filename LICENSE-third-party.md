**Usage in this project**: A subset of 20 vehicle brand logo images (thumb variants) from
the `filippofilip95/car-logos-dataset` repository have been downloaded and bundled as
SVG-wrapped assets under `packages/features/src/shared/vehicle-brand-logos/assets/`.
Only brands present in the Mexico-market `VEHICLE_BRAND_MODEL` dataset (spec 016) are
included. The Chirey brand uses the Chery mark from the upstream dataset (same
manufacturer). No modifications have been made to the image content.
